_onPing

  • _onPing(oldContacts: KBucketPeer[], newContact: KBucketPeer): void

  • Called on the ping event from k-bucket when a bucket is full and cannot split.

    oldContacts.length is defined by the numberOfNodesToPing param passed to the k-bucket constructor.

    oldContacts will not be empty and is the list of contacts that have not been contacted for the longest.
